About us

The objective of this webpage is to help create and raise awareness of people on understanding the current situation of our country's wildlife. As of 2022, many animals in our country are already consider endangered species and are moving towards extinction. The situation is very serious and requires the attention of the community. Therefore, this webpage is not only created to share info to raise awarenss, but also allows the webpage visitors to donate to help Malaysia's wildlife.
